ABOUT ME



John Weller is an American rapper, singer, guitarist and a record producer. I was born and raised in Lake Grove, New York on Long Island. I am popularly known for my single “High on You” released in the year 2010. I have released 10 mixtapes and 5 studio albums of which “The Suitation” is my lastest release dropped on March 10, 2020. My first studio album release was Worst Things You, on Jan 06, 2002.
